Who We Are

Redefining Property Management for Modern India

Founded in 2018, Stackly was born from a simple frustration — property management software was either too complex, too expensive, or built for Western markets. We set out to change that.

Today we power 3,200+ properties across 18 cities, from single apartments to large commercial parks. Our platform combines smart automation with a clean, mobile-first interface that anyone can master in one afternoon.
