Frequently Asked

Questions, Answered

Everything property managers and owners ask before getting started with Stackly.

Still have questions?

Our team replies in under 2 hours, Mon–Sat.

How long does it take to get my portfolio set up?

Most managers go live the same day. You can import properties in bulk via CSV or add them one at a time with our guided flow — no technical setup, no onboarding fees.

Is my data secure and RERA compliant?

Yes. Stackly uses bank-grade encryption, role-based access and automated audit trails. Our compliance module keeps your RERA filings, disclosures and pricing displays up to date.

Can tenants and buyers use it on mobile?

Absolutely. The tenant and buyer portal runs in any browser on phone, tablet or desktop — no app download required. They can pay rent, raise tickets, book site visits and access documents.

What does it cost, and is there a free trial?

Plans scale with your portfolio size, starting free for your first few units. There's no credit card needed to start, no lock-in contract, and you can upgrade or cancel any time.

Do you support commercial and mixed-use properties?

Yes — from single studios to gated villa communities and multi-tower commercial parks. Stackly handles residential, commercial and mixed-use portfolios under one login.
