FAQ

Common Questions

Is Stackly suitable for small landlords with just 2–3 properties?

Absolutely. Our Starter plan is designed for individuals managing 1–25 units. Many customers start with 2 properties and scale up. The platform is equally powerful at any size.

How long does setup take?

Most customers are fully live within one afternoon. We provide a free guided onboarding call, CSV import for your property list, and video tutorials for every feature. No technical knowledge required.

Is my data safe? Where is it stored?

All data is stored on AWS Mumbai servers (India data residency). We use AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. We are SOC 2 Type II audited and RERA registered. Daily backups retained for 90 days.

Can I migrate from my current software?

Yes — we support import from Excel, Google Sheets, and most major PMS platforms. Our onboarding team handles migration for Professional and Enterprise customers at no charge.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes, all plans include a free 30-day trial with full feature access. No credit card required. After 30 days, choose the plan that fits or cancel with one click.

Does Stackly support multiple cities?

Yes. Stackly is currently active in 18 cities across India including Chennai, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Mumbai, Pune, Delhi NCR and more. Multi-city portfolios are fully supported from a single dashboard.
